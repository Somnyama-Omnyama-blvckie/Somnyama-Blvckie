Our Services

Option 1: Traditional Farming

Direct or physical pig/s purchase:

We welcome all our customers to purchase pigs directly at our farm, our professional & friendly staff members help you to choose, scale and buy pig/s of your choice. Your affordability or pig price value is determined by the scale results (in Kgs) of a pig/s you chose to purchase. Under this option you can only be a once-off or regular customer.

Remember, our prices remain most affordable and competitive against all of the other pig farms in KZN.

Option 2: Metaverse farming

Opting for Metaverse farming automatically transform our customers to long term partners:

How?

  • Under this option you have the privilege and benefit of purchasing and owning a pig/s virtually in our digital Metaverse Piggy Farm.
  • This option is risk free, as we farm on your behalf, take care of all losses, needs & demands of growing livestock to sell for profit gain.
  • We feed, insure, vaccinate and regularly clean the pigs at our own expense as standard farming health-procedure. Without changing our partner customers extra service costs.

In every pig that we sell you get your monthly portion of revenue that is determined by the amount of virtual pig/s purchased.

How do you benefit?

  • When you purchase one virtual pig for once off R2200, 00. You receive a minimum of R500, 00 monthly revenue for a 12 months period only. There are more buying options available under the client portal section. You are not only limited to buying one pig, a single customer is allowed to buy from a minimum of 1 to a maximum of 50 virtual pigs per year. Alternatively, you can buy more pigs in proxy by appointing a person you trust to register under their name, in order to increase your number of virtual pig/s ownership.

How do we make money to generate substantial and stable revenue?

  • We focus on large scale white farm pigs breeding in order to stay ahead of our competitors, increase sales and generate sufficient profit gain, and uninterrupted supply to all our clients. That merge from individual pork consumers, small scale/ start-up farmers & large pork product producers. We work diligently to increase our market through reaching out via different available communication & marketing platforms.
  • Our Pig husbandry process sources the company with exponential drift increase twice per year: e.g. a single white female pig produces a maximum of six piglets twice a year. The abundance of piglets born from each female pig amongst thousand others. Efficiently ascertain our business stability. Thus, enable the company to designate each pig virtually to all Metaverse farm customers. Without running a risk of facing financial loss due to unforeseeable circumstances.
  • The company profits generously from surplus piglets' birth, & enjoys uncapped profit. Deservingly as we face all the risks, demands and payments for the business proceedings and necessities. While ensuring our customers' reception of guaranteed revenue.
